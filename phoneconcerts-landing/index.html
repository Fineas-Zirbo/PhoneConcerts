PhoneConcerts

PhoneConcerts is a Video Streaming App dedicated to live concerts. The app is a digital alternative to the standard, in-person, concert experience.

By using cutting edge technology, the online broadcasting of concerts is made accessible and attractive to mobile device users.

The viewer can stream live or pre-recorded concerts, swipe between camera angles, zoom in and out, create virtual concert souvenirs, edit them, and share them on Social Media.
